# FOUNDER_VISION.md

## What This App Is

A Chief of Staff for Vidhi Vakil.

Not a task manager.
Not a spreadsheet.
Not a dashboard.
Not project management software.

A system that tells the founder what to do next, why it matters, and what happens if she doesn't.

## The Experience

Vidhi opens the app at 7:00 AM.

In 30 seconds she knows:
- What is blocking SVAAS right now
- What decision needs to be made today
- Who she needs to talk to
- What single task moves the needle most
- What opportunity is within reach

She takes one action. The app celebrates it.

She comes back tomorrow because yesterday's action created visible progress.

## Core Loop

See → Decide → Act → Win → Return

Not: See → Analyze → Plan → Organize → Forget

## What Makes a Founder Come Back for 365 Days

1. The app knows what matters TODAY (not generally)
2. Every card has a button (not just information)
3. Consequences are visible (not hidden in spreadsheets)
4. Wins are celebrated (not just counted)
5. The app gets simpler over time (not more complex)

## Anti-Patterns (Never Do These)

- Show 358 tasks in a list
- Display "0/358 completed"
- Show empty metrics
- Add charts without actions
- Create pages without buttons
- Show information without consequences
- Build features the founder didn't ask for
